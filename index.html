WHAT ELSE TO EXPLORE IN
Yercaud
Emerald Lake
The most alluring aspect of Yercaud is its big yet natural lake very popularly known as the Emerald Lake.
Kiliyur Falls
The Kiliyur Falls drops from a height of 300 feet and are a part of the Shervaroyan hill range of the Eastern Ghats.
Botanical Garden
The National Orchidarium which is located 2 km from the Emerald Lake has a wide variety of orchids which includes 30 orchid species that are exclusively found here.
Bear's Cave
The cave is believed to be created by the bears and according to the primitive people of this surrounding, the cave also served as a secret hideout and escape route to the mighty ruler Tipu Sultan.
Shevaroy Hills
Perched at a height of 1620m. Boasting of magnificent views of lush green meadows, towering trees, dense woods and mighty hills.
